Lemony Steamed Fish

Description

Make mild-flavored, flaky fish fillets with this steamed fish recipe. Any type of fish will work, and you can increase the recipe easily. Try halibut, cod, salmon, red snapper, trout, etc. Serve with a white and wild rice blend.

  • 1 tablespoon dried dill weed
